RULES FOR THE GAME:

  • PRESS SPACEBAR CONTINUOUSLY TO ALLOW THE BIRD FLY
  • ONCE YOU LEAVE THE SPACEBAR BUTTON,THE BIRD STARTS FALLING-_-
  • ESCAPE THE HURDLES WITHOUT COLLIDING
  • GAME ENDS IF YOU COLLIDE WITH THE TWO PILLARS
  • GAME ENDS IF YOU STRIKE THE BOTTOM OR TOP OF THE BOX
  • SPEED OF THE GAME INCREASES WITH SCORE
  • ONCE GAME ENDS,CLICK ON RESTART TO PLAY AGAIN
